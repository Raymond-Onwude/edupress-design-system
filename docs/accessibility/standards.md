# EduPress Accessibility Standards

1. Every interactive control must have an accessible name.
2. Every form field must have a persistent label.
3. Keyboard focus must always remain visible.
4. Colour must not be the sole indicator of meaning.
5. Text and controls must meet WCAG contrast requirements.
6. Motion must respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
7. Documents and applications must declare their language.
8. Touch targets should be at least 44 by 44 CSS pixels for pupil interfaces.
9. Status changes must be exposed to assistive technology where appropriate.
10. Assessments must remain usable without audio-only instructions.
